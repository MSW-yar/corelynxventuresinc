Why CoreLynx

We're not building demos

Most agencies show you a chatbot on a landing page. Every agent we build has already been tested against a real operation first — CoreLynx runs its own e-commerce business, and that's where every design gets proven.

Our background is in credit and risk analysis as much as software, which shows up in how carefully an agent is scoped before it touches your customers or your money.

Every engagement includes

  • Scoping call and fixed setup quote — no open-ended hourly guessing
  • A working version to react to before final handoff
  • Hosting and monitoring included in the monthly rate
  • A monthly support allowance for iteration, not a one-time handoff
24/7 monitoring on every deployed agent
