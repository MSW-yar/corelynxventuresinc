About us

Your partner in building an online business

CoreLynx Ventures Inc. is an NYC-based e-commerce and dropshipping company that specializes in helping businesses launch and scale their online retail presence.

We work with both independent sellers and established physical businesses — including retail stores, specialty grocers, and niche product shops — who want to expand beyond their local area and reach customers online. Our team handles everything from initial feasibility assessment to full platform setup and ongoing sales analytics.

What we handle for you

  • Initial feasibility assessment & platform selection
  • Full account setup on Amazon, eBay, and Shopify
  • Product listing creation and optimization
  • Ongoing sales analytics and performance reporting
  • Competitive pricing strategy and market research
  • Business intelligence dashboards and data reporting
100% end-to-end support — from setup to ongoing operations

What we do

Our services

A complete suite of e-commerce services designed to get your business online and keep it growing.

Digital storefront setup

We help brick-and-mortar businesses and independent sellers launch fully operational online stores on Amazon, eBay, and Shopify — from account setup to listing optimization.

E-commerce feasibility assessment

Before going online, we analyze your inventory, sales records, and pricing to determine which products have the strongest potential and which platforms are the right fit.

Market research & trend analysis

We identify trending products and market opportunities across e-commerce platforms to help clients make data-driven decisions about what to sell and when.

Competitive pricing strategy

We analyze market pricing and historical invoice data to help clients price competitively across online channels without sacrificing margin.

Sales analytics & reporting

Ongoing analysis of sales performance, conversion metrics, and revenue trends to help clients understand what's working and where to optimize.

Business intelligence dashboards

We build and maintain reporting dashboards that give clients a clear, real-time picture of how their online business is performing at any given time.

Who we work with

Built for businesses like yours

Whether you're just getting started or ready to scale, we work with all kinds of businesses looking to build a real online presence.
